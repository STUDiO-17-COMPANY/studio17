Frequently Asked Questions

Are these candles or decorative gifts?

They are both! While they are fully functional candles made with premium wax and wicks, they are primarily designed as long-lasting decorative gifts that look beautiful and scent a room without ever needing to be lit.

Can I customise colours?

Yes, absolutely. Since every bouquet is handmade to order, we can mix custom colors to match a specific theme, event, or the recipient's favorite shades. Just let us know in your message.

How do I order?

Simply browse our catalogue, choose what you like, and click the "Order this bouquet" button. It will take you to our Instagram page where you can send us a direct message to confirm your order details personally.

Do you offer delivery or pickup?

We offer both! We can arrange secure local delivery to your door or the recipient's address, or you can choose to pick up your order directly from our studio.

How long does a custom order take?

Standard catalogue bouquets usually take 2-3 days to prepare. For heavily customised requests, please allow 5-7 days. We always recommend ordering a little early for special dates!
