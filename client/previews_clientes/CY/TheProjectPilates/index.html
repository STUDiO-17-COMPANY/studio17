First Time?

Everything you need to know before joining us.

Do I need prior experience?

Not at all. We welcome all levels. If you are entirely new, let your instructor know before class, and they will ensure you receive the proper modifications and guidance.

What should I wear?

Wear comfortable, form-fitting athletic wear that allows you to move freely. Grip socks are mandatory for all classes for hygiene and safety. You can bring your own or purchase a pair at the studio.

How early should I arrive?

Please arrive 10 minutes before your scheduled class time to get settled, store your belongings, and prepare for your session.
