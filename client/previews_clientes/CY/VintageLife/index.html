Saving the planet from waste, one hanger at a time.

Vintage Life is not just a clothing store; it's a manifesto against disposable culture. We were born in Nicosia with a clear mission: to give clothes a second life and actively combat the pollution generated by fast fashion.

We believe true exclusivity doesn't come from an assembly line. It's in the history of a piece, the flawless quality of vintage fabrics, and the art of restoration. We source unique pieces globally and carefully restore them by hand in our studio, preserving their essence and character.

What you see is what you get. No piece is ordinary. No restocks.
